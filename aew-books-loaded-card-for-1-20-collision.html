AEW Books Loaded Card For 1/20 Collision

September 12, 2024
blog

AEW brought big episode for the January 17th episode of AEW Dynamite. Before the show went off the air, they also booked matches for Collision this week.

AEW Collision goes down every Saturday on TNT. We will have play-by-play coverage of the show when the time comes. Now, we know what to expect this week.

The AEW Collision card includes a tag team match featuring Claudio and Bryan Danielson against Eddie Kingston and Ortiz. That match could be quite a showcase of their skills.

Another bout on the card is between Buddy Matthews and Daniel Garcia. Additionally, the event marks the return of Jon Moxley. The former AEW Champion hasn’t been seen for a while on Collision, so this should be a treat.

We will have full coverage of AEW Collision here at Ringside News. You never know what will break next, especially when Tony Khan keeps booking matches.

AEW Collision Card For 1/20/24

Claudio & Bryan Danielson vs Eddie Kingston & Ortiz

Buddy Matthews vs Daniel Garcia

Jon Moxley Returns
