CelebMix Awards 2017 nominees revealed!

July 15, 2024
blog

It’s finally time for our annual CelebMix Awards and we are now revealing the nominees.

Like last year, the CelebMix Awards 2017 will consist of two rounds. Round One starts tomorrow, December 1, at 12 pm UK time, and it will be open until December 10, at 8 pm UK time.

After this, the four nominees with the most votes in each category will continue on to Round Two, which begins on December 11, at 12 pm UK time. Voting for the CelebMix Awards will end on December 22, at 8 pm UK time and we will reveal the lucky winners the following day.
