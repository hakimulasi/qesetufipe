HBO Max releases trailer for new Selena Gomez cooking show

August 27, 2024
blog

HBO Max has unveiled the trailer for Selena + Chef, the brand new cooking show featuring global pop sensation Selena Gomez.

Filmed at Selena’s home during the COVID-19 pandemic, the show follows the songstress as she tries to enhance her culinary skills through lessons via video call from her famous chef friends.

A self proclaimed food lover, the trailer sees Selena admit that she’s not the best cook however, that doesn’t stop her from giving it her best shot. Whilst there are some close shaves and quite a few burnt offerings, it’s clear to see that Selena learns some valuable lessons along the way whilst also having a lot of fun.

Joining Selena on the show will be chefs Angelo Sosa, Antonia Lofaso, Candice Kumai, Daniel Holzman, Jon & Vinny, Ludo Lefebyre, Nancy Silverton, Nyesha Arrington, Roy Choi, and Tanya Holland.

Check out the trailer for Selena + Chef below.

Speaking about the show, Selena said: “Having some of the best chefs open up their kitchens to me was a humbling and fun experience. I definitely discovered I have a lot more to learn. I’m also really happy that we were able to highlight and raise money for some incredible charitable organizations.”

“Watching Selena with these incredible chefs has been a delicious joy,” added Sarah Aubrey, head of original content at HBO Max. “You don’t need to be an experienced chef yourself to enjoy the show; you learn with her and get to see all the fun that happens in the kitchen. Try not to watch it while hungry!”

Whether you love food, Selena, or simply relate to not being a great chef, this is one TV show you’re definitely going to want to binge-watch.

Selena + Chef will be able to stream on HBO Max from August 13.

Selena + Chef is Selena’s latest project, following the announcement of her new cosmetics line Rare Beauty, her ‘Past Life’ collaboration with Trevor Daniel, and the release of the deluxe edition of her latest album Rare earlier this year.
