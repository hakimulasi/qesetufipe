Career

Balfe started her modeling career when she was spotted by an agent at a charity event in Dublin. Awards & Achievements

For her role as Claire Fraser in Outlander, she has won two Saturn Awards, two People’s Choice Awards; she has also received four Golden Globe Award nominations in the category for Best Actress in a Television Series – Drama. She has won an Irish Film and Television Award too.
