Early Life and Education

Angela Cheng was born on September 9, 1959, in Hong Kong. She immigrated to Edmonton, Alberta, Canada, as a child, which marked the beginning of her journey into the world of music.

Angela began her formal music education at Alberta College, where she studied piano under Vera Shean.

She continued her studies at the University of Alberta with Ernesto Lejano, further honing her skills and preparing for a career that would eventually take her to prestigious stages around the world.
