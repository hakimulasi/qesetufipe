Early Life

Born on August 18, 1992, in Los Angeles, California, she was named after the guitarist of the Scottish indie-pop duo, The Vaselines, Frances McKee. Her father, Kurt, gave her the middle name Bean because he felt she looked like a kidney bean in an ultrasound.

A lot of investigations took place and eventually absolved both parents of the blame. The last time Cobain saw her father alive was in 1994 when she visited him in rehab. He was found dead soon after her visit.

She was raised by her mother, her aunts, and her paternal grandmother. She grew up in L.A. and Seattle. Her grandmother was given custody of her when her mother overdosed on painkillers. Love regained custody in 2005.
