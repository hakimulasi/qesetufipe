Everything you need to know about Ava Phillipe 

    Ava Phillippe, the daughter of Hollywood stars Reese Witherspoon and Ryan Phillippe is making waves in the fashion and entertainment industry. 

    She’s collaborated with big-name brands, has over a million followers on Instagram, and is known for attending some of the coolest events. Here’s what you need to know about this rising star.

    Who is Ava dating?

    Ava was currently dating fellow UC Berkeley student Owen Mahoney. While the duo kept their relationship largely offline, it's unclear if they are still together . 

    After coming out as pansexual, Ava shared an important message to her millions of followers, saying: “I can [and] will block profiles commenting bigoted/hateful messages under my posts. My Instagram profile is not a place for cruelty/hate speech [and] I intend to keep it that way.”

    What does Ava do?

    Ava has collaborated with big-name brands such as Beyoncé’s Ivy Park and Pat McGrath Labs, proving her ability to keep up with the hottest trends in the fashion industry. In April, she attended Coachella parties hosted by The Zoe Report’s ZOEasis and H&M, showing her passion for fashion and entertainment.

    What are Ava's hobbies?

    Apart from her fashion sense, Ava is also a talented artist. She’s showcased her artwork on Instagram, including sketches of herself, her mother, and even her beloved dog, Benji. Ava sells her artwork on Society6 and Etsy, where she donates a portion of her proceeds to charity.

    Has Ava worked with her mom Reese Witherspoon?

    Despite having famous parents in the entertainment industry, Ava is focused on pursuing her own path. 

    She’s a student at the University of California, Berkeley, and became a face of Amazon's Off to College campaign in 2019. While she’s expressed interest in pursuing acting, she’s taking her time to figure out what path is right for her.
