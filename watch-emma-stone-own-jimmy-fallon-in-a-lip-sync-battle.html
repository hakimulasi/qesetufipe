Watch Emma Stone own Jimmy Fallon in a lip sync battle!

July 17, 2024
blog

Jimmy Fallon has faced some stiff competition during his always enjoyable lip sync battle segment but he very well may have faced his toughest yet as actress Emma Stone proved to be a stone cold killer on the fake mic!

Emma was on the show promoting her upcoming summer blockbuster The Amazing Spider-Man 2 but it was the following throw down that’s getting all the buzz – and for good reason.

Fallon started off with “Fancy” by Iggy Azalea which was fun but Stone went off the hook as she blazed through Blue’s Traveler’s “Hook.” Fallon was literally floored during the inspired performance and stated, “I can’t even talk to you right now. I forgot all about that song. So good! I really gotta pick it up now. I’ve gotta up my game.”

For round 2 an intimidated and obviously shaken Fallon tried to rally with Styx’s “Mr. Roboto” but it was already too late as Stone closed the house down with an on point go at DJ Khaled’s “All I Do Is Win.”

For my taste there’s nothing more consistently entertaining right now than Fallon’s video segment skits and this Emma beat down did nothing to alter that opinion.

Great stuff!
