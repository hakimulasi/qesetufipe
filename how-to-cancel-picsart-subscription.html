How do I cancel my PicsArt subscription?

To Cancel your PicsArt subscription on our website:
  • Go to www.picsart.com.
  • Log in to your PicsArt account.
  • Click on your profile photo in the top right corner of the screen, then select Settings.
  • Go to Billing & Subscriptions and click Unsubscribe.
