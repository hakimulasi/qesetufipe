Kylie Bunbury is a Canadian-born American actress. Her roles include the films Prom (2011), The Sitter, Game Night, and Warning, and the series Twisted, Under the Dome, Tut, Pitch, When They See Us, Brave New World, and Big Sky.

Kylie is the daughter of Kristi and Alex Bunbury, a professional soccer player. Her father is from Plaisance, Guyana. Her mother is white, from Minnesota, U.S., and has one half Swedish, one quarter Polish, as well as German and English, ancestry. Kylie was raised in England, Madeira in Portugal, and Prior Lake, Minnesota. Among her siblings are professional soccer players Teal Bunbury and Mataeo Bunbury. Kylie is married to Jon-Ryan Alan Riggins, with whom she has a son.

Kylie’s maternal grandfather is Ronald Delano Novak (the son of Sylvester Stanley Novak and Clara T. Anderson). Ronald was born in Minnesota. Sylvester was son of Polish parents, Andrew C. Nowak and Stanislawa/Stella Kalma. Clara was the daughter of Axel Anderson, who was born in Sweden, and of Hellen Swenson, who was born in Minnesota, to Swedish parents.

Kylie’s maternal grandmother is Claudia Lou Carlson (the daughter of Guy Albin Carlson and Myrtle Catherine Hartman). Claudia was born in Minnesota. Guy was the son of Swedish parents, Peter S. Carlson and Hannah/Hanna C. Nelson. Myrtle was the daughter of Allan Edward Hartman and Catherine Louise “Cassie” Weyer/Dickey.
