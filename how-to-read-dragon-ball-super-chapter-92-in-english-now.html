How to read Dragon Ball Super Chapter 92 in English now

As Dragon Ball Super‘s latest arc reaches its initial fighting grounds, the so-called Super Hero arc started in Chapter 88, with a prequel arc anticipating the events that will follow in the second half of the story arc. Its predecessor, the Granolah arc, lasted for about 20 chapters until effectively reaching its ending in Chapter 87 with the battle between the Saiyans and Gas finally reaching a grand conclusion.

The current mini-arc, made up of chapters 90 and 91 – which covers the first few minutes of Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero – reached a conclusion, allowing the Super Hero arc to finally continue as expected. Up until yesterday, only spoilers derived from raw scans (i.e. untranslated) were available, spoiling many fans about what the future might entail for Piccolo and his altercation with Gamma 2. That was until the official English translation finally surfaced. Here is where you can finally catch up with Dragon Ball Super.

Where to read Dragon Ball Super Chapter 92?

The newest chapter of Dragon Ball Super is available on MangaPlus by SHUEISHA, the partner website working with the Shōnen Jump magazine, VIZ. The chapter reached the platform just a few hours ago with its official English translation as a part of the monthly V-Jump issue alongside the newest chapters of Boruto: The Next Generation, One Piece, and Chainsaw Man. Moreover, Dragon Ball Super reaches the magazine on the 21st of every month, available to read on the website, or alternatively, on Shueisha’s Shōnen Jump Plus app.

Naturally, escaping the spoilers seems an almost impossible task, especially as independent and unofficial translators take on the task of jumping on the newest chapters for all impatient fans who wish to read the story as quickly as possible. Nonetheless, the official translations are normally only a day behind, providing an official translation and source for all fans of the Shōnen series.

The next chapter will be released on May 21.
