Adam Sandler has three pretty ladies at home that look as if they could pass for sisters.

On Sunday, the comedian was joined by his 49-year-old wife Jackie Sandler as well as their two daughters Sadie, 17, and Sunny, 15, at the premiere of Netflix's Leo at the Regency Village Theater in Westwood, California.

The women were all in fancy dresses as they stormed the brown carpet while Adam, as usual, dressed way down.

All three ladies voice characters in the animated movie that has Adam as star. Sandler talked to People about what a great time he has working with clan: 'It’s always fun to be with my family,' noted the Hollywood veteran.

And the 57-year-old is happy his teen girls are following in his footsteps: 'It's kind of what they are thinking about getting into and their passion, and so it's good to learn with them.'

Sadie first was seen on screen in her dad's 2008 movie You Don't Mess With The Zohan; she played the Girl Riding On Goat.

Then she popped up as the Sweetest Medieval Girl Of All Time in 2008's Bedtime Stories and was Tardio's daughter in Grown Ups.

In 2011's smash hit comedy Just Go With It with Aniston, Sadie played a girl on the rope bridge.

That same year she was the little girl on the ship in Jack And Jill.

In 2012 she was the lemonade stand kid in That's My Boy and had the voice of young Mavis in Hotel Transylvania (Selena Gomez played the teen Mavis).

In 2013 she played Sadie Tardio in Grown Ups 2 and the next year she was a little league announcer in Blended.

In 2015 she popped in three films: Pixels, Hotel Transylvania 2 and The Ridiculous 6.

After then she was in The Do-Over, Sandy Wexler, The Week Of, and Hotel Transylvania 3 over the next few years.

In 2019 she played Summer in Murder Mystery with Aniston.

After that she was seen in The Wrong Missy and Hubie Halloween as Danielle.

In her next film, You Are SO Not Invited to My Bat Mitzvah! she will have a bigger role as Ronnie Friedman.

And she will voice a character in the 2023 film Leo.

In September it was revealed that You Are SO Not Invited to My Bat Mitzvah! will also star Sadie's sister Sunny as well as Adam's wife Jackie.

You Are SO Not Invited To My Bat Mitzvah will reunite Sandler with his costar Idina Menzel, who played his wife in his critically acclaimed thriller Uncut Gems.

Traffic actor Luis Guzmán will round out the adult cast members.

The film will be directed by Sammi Cohen, with Alison Peck handling the adapted screenplay. 

Sadie and Sunny are set to both earn $65,000 on their father's coming-of-age comedy, according to legal documents obtained by The Blast.

Although the film's roles haven't officially been released yet, the documents also appear to confirm that Sadie will star as the lead character Ronnie, while Sunny will play her sister Stacy.
